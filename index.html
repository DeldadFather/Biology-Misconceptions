Biology Myths

Scroll to uncover the truth behind common misconceptions.

Myth 01

Blood is Blue

Blood is never blue. It is always red. Deoxygenated blood is a deep, dark red, but light filtering through your skin makes veins appear blue.

Myth 02

The 10% Brain Rule

You use virtually every part of your brain. Brain imaging shows that even during sleep, almost all regions are active at some level.

Myth 03

Sugar Causes Hyperactivity

Scientific studies have repeatedly failed to find a link between sugar intake and hyperactive behavior in children.

Myth 04

Evolution is "Progress"

Evolution doesn't have an end goal of "complexity." It simply favors whatever traits help an organism survive in its specific environment.

Myth 05

Goldfish Memory

Goldfish actually have impressive memories. They can remember things for months and can even be trained to respond to colors and music.
